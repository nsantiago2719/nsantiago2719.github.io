the As a Code

Since DevOps is on the rise for the past years and still continuing to the years to come I've encountered the words like "Infrastructre as a Code" or even "Configuration as a Code". My day to day also involves some the tools for this like Terraform and Cloudformation which is a tool for "Infrastructure as a Code. I admit that Terraform is easier than Cloudformation but there are use cases I guess. On the future I would like to use some tools like Chef, Puppet or Ansible as they for "Configuration as a Code" not for "Infrastructure as a Code".

DevOps Beliefs & Philosophy

Through out the years that I have been working immersed on the word DevOps that I it have as my career, I have encountered some misconceptions and bad practices about it that goes different from what I've read and learned. In my opinion and perspective, for the company to have a "true DevOps" implemented they should have single common goal which brings to my Philosophies and Beliefs.

Philosophies
  • the Team should have a once common goal. Which is to run and serve a service that has a good quality for the end-user.
  • teamwork should be observed and strictly must have.
  • never forget to always learn and improve the existing process/system/culture.
Beliefs
  • in my own definition, DevOps is the by product of the company's effort to deliver the good quality product to their end-users.
  • to add, by aiming to have "true DevOps" it will grant the company a big change that can improve its performance.
  • lastly, once a "true DevOps" is achieved the end-users will be delighted for the quality of the service they are receiving.
